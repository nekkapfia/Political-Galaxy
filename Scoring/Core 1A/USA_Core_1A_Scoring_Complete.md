## 1. Rule & Methodology Changes (Authoritative & Locked)

### Current-Policy Basis (Locked Rule)
All scoring for USA ideologies, leaders, and figures is performed on **current observable policies** (what they are actively maintaining or promoting in the real world). Aspirational or "unconstrained total control" goals are **not** used unless explicitly instructed by the user for a specific entity. This ensures full methodological equivalence with the UK and Germany documents. (This rule was explicitly applied in the past 10 responses to align USA Progressives/MAGA with UK equivalents.)

### Strict Negative-Only Weighting (Locked Rule)
Scores reflect only the presence and weight of invasive/restrictive policies. Removal, opposition, or repeal of restrictions grants **no positive credit**. "We will repeal X" or "we oppose Y" is rhetoric only and does not raise the score.

### Guantanamo / Enemy Combatants = Core 2B Boundary (Locked Clarification)
Policies related to Guantanamo Bay (indefinite detention of foreign nationals as “enemy combatants”, enhanced interrogation techniques, denial of habeas corpus, executive designation as enemy combatant, etc.) are classified under **Core 2B** (external enemy combatants captured and held extraterritorially) and have been **excluded from all Core 1A leader scores**. These individuals are not "living under" domestic sovereign rule in the sense required for Core 1A measurement. Bush and Obama scores were recalculated accordingly after this clarification. This is a USA-specific boundary rule.

### Bathroom Bills / GAC Bans / Sex-Realist Policies Caveat (Locked)
Treated as ideologically contested. The protection-of-vulnerable caveat exists in the policy document (acknowledging statistical/sociological evidence on male-pattern risk for female-only spaces) but is **inactive** until the debate is resolved. Currently scored as ideological enforcement (44 range) unless the specific exception for sex-segregated spaces applies. Both female-only spaces and trans-inclusive spaces can qualify for the caveat in principle (data supports protection of vulnerable in both framings), but the caveat remains inactive for scoring purposes.

### No Positive Buffs for Repeal/Opposition (Locked)
Repealing or opposing previous restrictions is never scored as a positive factor. Only active, maintained invasive policies lower the score.

### Endos/Xenos Split (Applied Selectively)
Applied where explicit permanent legal divergence between in-group (Endos) and out-group (Xenos) treatment exists (e.g., certain immigration or citizenship rules, or differential treatment in specific policies). For mainstream ideologies/leaders, differential treatment is noted in rationales but does not always trigger separate holistic scores unless the divergence is structurally significant.

## 3. All Scored Policies — Full Entries with Expanded Justifications

### Foundational / Surveillance Policies (USA-specific implementations)

#### Patriot Act / Bulk Data Collection & Warrantless Wiretapping (Bush/Obama era expansions)
**Neutered Base Policy:** Expansion of surveillance authorities allowing bulk collection of communications metadata and content (with incidental collection on US persons) without individualised warrants in many contexts; mass data retention and investigatory powers.  
**Core 1A Score:** 29–30  
**Rationale:** Police-state / institutional surveillance element. Systematic, suspicionless or broadly authorised collection of personal data. Mechanically similar to UK RIPA/DRIPA. Fits the 28–36 ideological enforcement / police-state band. (Guantanamo elements excluded as Core 2B.)

#### General Police and Criminal Investigation Powers (with post-9/11 expansions)
**Neutered Base Policy:** Standard procedural powers of search, seizure, interrogation, arrest, detention and limited surveillance in criminal investigations, subject to reasonable suspicion, judicial warrants and proportionality requirements, with post-9/11 expansions in some areas.  
**Core 1A Score:** 85  
**Rationale:** Routine criminal justice tools for prevention of harm to others. Extreme procedural limitations (judicial oversight, rights protections, proportionality) place it firmly in the protection-of-vulnerable / prevention-of-harm band.

### Progressive Identity Package (USA implementations — consistent with UK)

#### Mandatory EDI / Sensitivity / Unconscious Bias Training (across institutions, corporations, education, government)
**Neutered Base Policy:** Requirement for institutions, corporations, education, and government bodies to conduct mandatory training sessions that instruct participants their existing beliefs on identity are biased/harmful and must be corrected.  
**Core 1A Score:** 22  
**Rationale:** Direct ideological retraining / thought control. The single heaviest and most consistent downward driver across all progressive packages. Matches the 19–27 ideological reprogramming / thought control band. (Identical to UK component.)

#### Self-ID / Self-Declaration Gender Recognition (progressive model with minimal gatekeeping)
**Neutered Base Policy:** Legal recognition of acquired gender based on self-declaration with minimal or zero gatekeeping, medical evidence, or waiting periods; often linked to compulsory institutional recognition.  
**Core 1A Score:** 44  
**Rationale:** Early ideological/identity-based control. Creates institutional rules requiring conformity in speech and self-presentation. Fits the 44–50 band. (Identical to UK component.)

#### Compulsory Institutional Recognition of Self-Declared Gender
**Neutered Base Policy:** Requirement for services, sports, prisons, workplaces, and official documents to treat self-declared gender as legal sex, often with limited or no exceptions (federal and institutional contexts).  
**Core 1A Score:** 42  
**Rationale:** Institutional compulsion and identity-based control. Strong downward driver when combined with EDI and self-ID. (Identical to UK component.)

#### Non-Crime Hate Incident Recording and Institutional Monitoring
**Neutered Base Policy:** Recording and institutional monitoring of incidents that are not criminal but are perceived as motivated by hostility toward protected characteristics, often leading to institutional alignment requirements.  
**Core 1A Score:** 26  
**Rationale:** Police-state monitoring of deviant behaviour and ideological enforcement. Fits the 19–27 / 28–36 range depending on enforcement intensity. (Identical to UK component.)

#### Expansion of Hate Crime / Expression Sanctioning Frameworks and Speech Codes
**Neutered Base Policy:** Expansion of hate crime laws, expression sanctioning frameworks, and institutional speech codes with significant penalties and institutional monitoring requirements.  
**Core 1A Score:** 24  
**Rationale:** Expanded expression sanctioning / new content moderation regime. State-mandated proactive censorship through corporate/institutional proxy. Fits the 19–27 ideological enforcement band.

### Gender-Affirming Care (GAC) / Self-ID Related Policies (USA context)

#### Restrictions on Gender-Affirming Care (GAC) for Minors + Significant Limits for Adults (MAGA-aligned policies)
**Neutered Base Policy:** Blanket or near-blanket restrictions on medical and surgical interventions for gender dysphoria in minors, with significant limits or requirements for adults; often tied to biological sex as the legal category and protection of female-only spaces.  
**Core 1A Score:** 82 (protection of vulnerable) or 44 (ideological enforcement while caveat inactive)

The caveat acknowledges statistical/sociological evidence supporting protection of vulnerable in both female-only spaces framing and trans-inclusive framing, but is not applied for scoring until the ideological debate is resolved. (Parallel to UK bathroom bills / GAC caveat.)

#### Retention and Legal Protection of Biological Female-Only Spaces (Sports, Prisons, Shelters, etc.)
**Neutered Base Policy:** Retention and legal protection of sex-segregated spaces based on biological sex, with explicit carve-outs or protections for female-only categories in sports, prisons, shelters, and similar contexts.  
**Core 1A Score:** 82 (when caveat active) or 44 (while caveat inactive)  
**Rationale:** Same as above — currently ideological enforcement (44) while caveat inactive. Data supports protection of vulnerable in the female-only spaces framing (male-pattern risk statistics), but the caveat is not applied until ideological contestation is resolved.
