# Core 1A: Eras of Personal Autonomy in Nazi Germany (1933–1945)

## Introductory Overview

Nazi Germany produced one of the most rapid and comprehensive collapses of personal autonomy in modern history. Within months of the seizure of power the regime dismantled residual Weimar protections and constructed a totalitarian system of speech, association, religious, movement and bodily controls. Designated Xenos groups (Jews, political opponents, Roma, homosexuals, Jehovah’s Witnesses, the disabled) faced intensifying differential treatment culminating in genocide. The Base/Endos score falls to the extreme low single digits; the Xenos score approaches the floor.

## **1933–1934: Seizure and Coordination – Endos 25 / Xenos 12**

Rapid destruction of federalism, parties, free press and independent associations.

### Core 1A Relevant Restrictions / Elements – Expanded Analysis
* Enabling Act and suspension of civil liberties.
* Banning of opposition parties and independent trade unions.
* Early concentration camps and press controls.
* Initial anti-Jewish measures.

**Key Shift:** Replacement of the Weimar constitutional order with the National Socialist monopoly.

### Why this produces Endos 25 / Xenos 12
Immediate multi-domain collapse with early differential targeting.

---

## **1935–1939: Full System – Endos 15 / Xenos 5**

Nuremberg Laws, complete Gleichschaltung, and intensification of racial and political controls.

### Core 1A Relevant Restrictions / Elements – Expanded Analysis
* Comprehensive racial legislation.
* Total control of media, culture and association.
* Escalating exclusion and violence against designated groups.

**Key Shift:** Codification of the racial state and completion of institutional coordination.

### Why this produces Endos 15 / Xenos 5
Further densification and radicalisation of the restrictive system.

---

## **1939–1945: Wartime and Genocide – Endos 8 / Xenos 2**

Total mobilisation, Night and Fog, and the implementation of the Final Solution.

### Core 1A Relevant Restrictions / Elements – Expanded Analysis
* Extreme wartime speech and movement controls.
* Genocide as the ultimate differential restriction on the targeted out-groups.
* Total mobilisation of society under Party and SS control.

**Key Shift:** Transition to open genocidal policy and total war mobilisation.

### Why this produces Endos 8 / Xenos 2
Maximum intensity of multi-domain and group-targeted coercion.
