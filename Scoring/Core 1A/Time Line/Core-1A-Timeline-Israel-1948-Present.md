# Core 1A: Eras of Personal Autonomy in Israel (1948–Present)

## Introductory Overview

Israel maintains a relatively high Core 1A baseline for its Jewish and core citizen population under a democratic system with strong judicial protections for speech and association, while applying a markedly more restrictive regime in the occupied territories and, at times, to Arab citizens under emergency or security frameworks. The trajectory shows continuity of a dual structure rather than a single linear liberalisation or tightening. COVID produced a temporary nationwide restriction. The overall citizen-level scores remain higher than most European comparators; the differential treatment of non-citizen populations in the territories is the principal source of any Endos/Xenos framing.

## **1948–1966: Founding and Military Government – Endos 70 / Territories 35**

Establishment of the state with robust democratic institutions for the Jewish population and military administration over most Arab citizens.

### Core 1A Relevant Restrictions / Elements – Expanded Analysis
* Strong formal speech and association rights for citizens inside the Green Line.
* Military government restrictions on movement, association and expression for Arab citizens until 1966.
* Core criminal prohibitions retained.

**Key Shift:** Creation of the dual legal-political structure.

### Why this produces Endos 70 / Territories 35
High citizen baseline with clear differential for the population under military rule.

---

## **1967–1987: Occupation Framework – Endos 72 / Territories 30**

Extension of Israeli control to the West Bank, Gaza, Sinai and Golan; development of the occupation legal regime.

### Core 1A Relevant Restrictions / Elements – Expanded Analysis
* Continued high autonomy for Israeli citizens.
* Comprehensive movement, association and expression controls in the territories.
* Settlement and security measures.

**Key Shift:** Institutionalisation of the post-1967 territorial regime.

### Why this produces Endos 72 / Territories 30
Slight upward drift in the citizen sphere; continued low scores under occupation.

---

## **1988–2000: Intifada and Oslo – Endos 74 / Territories 32**

First Intifada security measures followed by limited Oslo-era transfers of authority.

### Core 1A Relevant Restrictions / Elements – Expanded Analysis
* Temporary intensification of security controls.
* Partial relaxation in areas transferred to Palestinian Authority administration.
* Retention of the overall dual structure.

**Key Shift:** Oscillation within the established dual framework.

### Why this produces Endos 74 / Territories 32
Modest net movement.

---

## **2001–2019: Security Consolidation – Endos 70 / Territories 28**

Second Intifada, construction of the security barrier, and continued occupation administration.

### Core 1A Relevant Restrictions / Elements – Expanded Analysis
* Heightened security and movement controls in the territories.
* Retention of democratic speech and association rights for citizens.
* Periodic emergency regulations.

**Key Shift:** Re-assertion of comprehensive security control after the collapse of the Oslo process.

### Why this produces Endos 70 / Territories 28
Return to a slightly more restrictive equilibrium.

---

## **2020–early 2022: COVID Block – Endos 50 / Territories 25**

Nationwide emergency regulations on movement and gathering.

### Core 1A Relevant Restrictions / Elements – Expanded Analysis
* Lockdowns and gathering limits applied to the citizen population.
* Continued territorial regime.

**Key Shift:** Temporary nationwide emergency overlay.

### Why this produces Endos 50 / Territories 25
Protocol-consistent scoring of the COVID measures.

## **Mid-2022 onward: Post-COVID Continuity – Endos 68 / Territories 28**

Lifting of COVID measures with retention of the long-standing dual structure.

### Core 1A Relevant Restrictions / Elements – Expanded Analysis
* Termination of the most sweeping COVID restrictions.
* Ongoing occupation and security framework in the territories.
* High formal speech protections for citizens.

**Key Shift:** Removal of the temporary COVID layer.

### Why this produces Endos 68 / Territories 28
Recovery limited by the persistent dual structure.
