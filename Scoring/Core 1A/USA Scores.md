## 1. USA Political Ideologies — Locked Scores & Rationale

### Modern USA Progressives — Score: 29 (28–36 band)

**Primary Mechanical Drivers:**
- Mandatory Sensitivity / EDI / Unconscious Bias Training across institutions, corporations, education, and government (score component 22 — the single heaviest driver).
- Self-ID / Self-Declaration Gender Recognition with zero or minimal gatekeeping (score component ~44).
- Compulsory Institutional Recognition of Self-Declared Gender in services, sports, prisons, workplaces, etc. (score component ~42).
- Non-Crime Hate Incident Recording and institutional monitoring/alignment requirements (score components 26 and 24).
- Expansion of hate crime / expression sanctioning frameworks and speech codes.

**Why this score?**  
Scored on current observable policies (same constrained basis as UK Green Party / Zac Polanski and other UK progressive parties). The mandatory EDI/sensitivity training element (direct ideological retraining / thought control at 22) is the dominant downward driver. Combined with self-ID and institutional compulsion, this produces scores in the 28–36 band, matching the entire UK progressive cluster (29–30). The score reflects nationwide policy implementation rather than aspirational unconstrained goals.

### MAGA Movement — Score: 66 (60–69 band)

**Primary Mechanical Drivers:**
- Prohibition of self-ID; biological sex as the sole legal category in law and policy.
- Blanket or near-blanket restrictions on Gender-Affirming Care (GAC) for minors, with significant limits for adults.
- Strong retention and legal protection of biological female-only spaces (sports, prisons, shelters, etc.).
- Resistance to mandatory EDI/retraining and institutional ideological compulsion.
- Emphasis on free speech and reduced expansion of hate incident recording / speech codes.

**Why this score?**  
Scored on current observable policies (same constrained basis as UK Reform UK, Restore Britain, Farage, and Lowe). Strong and consistent opposition to the core low-scoring mechanisms of the progressive identity package (especially EDI training at 22 and self-ID compulsion). Places the movement in the same high band (60–69) as equivalent UK right-of-centre parties. Strict negative-only rule applied.

## 2. USA Political Leaders / Figures — Independent Core 1A Scores

This section provides full independent holistic scores and detailed rationales for every named USA political leader discussed in the thread. Each has their own unique score based on the specific combination of policies active during their tenure or advocacy. Scores are strictly negative (presence of invasive policies lowers the score; removal/opposition avoids the penalty but grants no positive bonus). Guantanamo-related policies are excluded from Core 1A as they fall under Core 2B (external enemy combatants captured and held extraterritorially, not integrated into domestic sovereign rule or "living under" the system).

**George W. Bush (2001–2009)** — Score: **41** (37–43 band)  
**Rationale:** Domestic Core 1A policies limited to surveillance expansions (Patriot Act bulk data collection, warrantless wiretapping programs with incidental collection on US persons — mechanically similar to RIPA/DRIPA at ~29–30, police state / institutional surveillance band). Guantanamo policies (indefinite detention, enhanced interrogation, habeas denial, etc.) excluded entirely as they belong under Core 2B (captured foreign enemy combatants held extraterritorially, not integrated into domestic sovereign rule or "living under" the system). No mandatory EDI/retraining or self-ID elements existed in this era. Score reflects only domestic invasiveness after Core 1A/2B clarification and recheck.

**Barack Obama (2009–2017)** — Score: **43** (37–43 band)  
**Rationale:** Continuation and institutionalisation of surveillance frameworks inherited from Bush (bulk collection, monitoring programs) with no major rollback of existing powers. Slightly higher than Bush due to less aggressive expansion of new surveillance authorities. Guantanamo policies excluded (Core 2B). Domestic focus remains police state / surveillance elements. Explicitly rechecked and recalculated after the Core 2B decision for Guantanamo.

**Joe Biden (2021–2025)** — Score: **30** (28–36 band)  
**Rationale:** Continuation and expansion of institutional EDI frameworks, support for self-ID elements, compulsory institutional recognition of self-declared gender in federal contexts, maintenance of non-crime hate incident recording and institutional monitoring requirements. Aligns directly with the Establishment Democrats ideology score. The combination of EDI training (22), self-ID elements, and institutional compulsion places it in the 28–36 band.

**Donald Trump (2017–2021 & 2025–current)** — Score: **66** (60–69 band)  
**Rationale:** Strong opposition to self-ID expansion, emphasis on biological sex as the sole legal category in law and policy, restrictions on Gender-Affirming Care for minors, retention and legal protection of biological female-only spaces (sports, prisons, shelters), and resistance to mandatory EDI/retraining and institutional ideological compulsion. Scored on current observable policies (same basis as UK Reform UK / Restore Britain / Farage / Lowe). Strict negative-only rule applied — no positive credit for opposition to or repeal of previous policies.

**Alexandria Ocasio-Cortez** — Score: **29** (28–36 band)  
**Rationale:** Strong advocacy for self-ID with minimal or zero gatekeeping, mandatory EDI/sensitivity/unconscious bias training across institutions, compulsory institutional gender recognition in all contexts, and expansion of hate crime / expression sanctioning frameworks. Scored on current observable policies (same basis as UK Green Party / Zac Polanski and other UK progressive parties). Exemplifies the heaviest drivers of the progressive identity package (especially ideological retraining at 22).

**Bernie Sanders (Sanders wing)** — Score: **29** (28–36 band)  
**Rationale:** Strong advocacy for self-ID with minimal or zero gatekeeping, mandatory EDI/sensitivity/unconscious bias training across institutions, compulsory institutional gender recognition, and expansion of institutional monitoring and speech-regulation frameworks. Scored on current observable policies (same basis as UK Green Party / Zac Polanski and other UK progressive parties). Exemplifies the heaviest drivers of the progressive identity package (especially ideological retraining at 22).
