## **1995–2012: Chirac–Sarkozy Continuity – Final Score: 29**

The Chirac and Sarkozy presidencies emphasised pragmatic governance, European integration, and reform for competitiveness.

### Moral Justification Sources – Expanded Analysis

- Policy was justified largely through arguments of economic necessity, European position, and governability.
- Republican values were invoked as cultural and institutional inheritance rather than as active transcendent commands.
- Security and immigration debates focused on practical order and social cohesion.
- Evidence and outcomes carried primary weight.

Mundane sources were dominant.

**Key Shift:** This era consolidated a strongly pragmatic mode of justification centred on economic and institutional performance.
