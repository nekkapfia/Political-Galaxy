## **2017–Present: Macron Era – Final Score: 33**

Emmanuel Macron’s presidencies have emphasised competence, reform, European leadership, and the defence of republican order as practical necessities.

### Moral Justification Sources – Expanded Analysis

- Major reforms are justified through arguments of economic competitiveness, institutional functionality, and long-term sustainability.
- Republican and laïque principles are defended as essential to social cohesion and state authority rather than as abstract higher law.
- Progressive and populist challenges are met primarily with claims of competence and order.
- Transcendent language is limited; pragmatic and institutional justifications predominate.

**Key Shift:** This era has reinforced a mundane-dominant style in which competence, reform, and republican order as practical goods supply the main sources of moral authority.
