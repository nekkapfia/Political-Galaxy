# Labour Party – Core 2A (National Sovereignty)

**Score: 45**

## Brief Overview
Mixed approach: stronger enforcement against illegal migration while prioritising continued international integration via ECHR and Windsor Framework.

- Reform asylum system and expand safe legal routes
- Maintain ECHR with improvements to removal interaction
- Keep Windsor Framework, explore reductions in impact on Great Britain
