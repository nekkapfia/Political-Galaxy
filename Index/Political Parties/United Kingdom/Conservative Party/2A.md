# Conservative Party – Core 2A (National Sovereignty)

**Score: 48**

## Brief Overview
Incremental improvements on illegal migration and removal capacity while retaining the core structural constraints of the ECHR and Windsor Framework. Modest overall increase in national sovereignty.

## Key Positions
- Strengthen border enforcement and increase deportations
- Reform (but not necessarily leave) the ECHR
- Maintain Windsor Framework with possible minor adjustments
- Reduce net legal migration through tighter visa rules

## Why 48
Incremental gains on enforcement offset by retention of ECHR and Windsor Framework constraints.
