## Brief Overview

The Green Party’s Core 1B position features large-scale renationalisation of utilities, expanded public investment vehicles, strong environmental mandates, and higher wealth taxation. This produces the lowest final score among major parties (48).

## Expanded Analysis

### Key Policy Movements (Regulation)

Large-scale public ownership, strong environmental mandates, and expanded state direction of investment and production pull the Regulation Score to 35 — the largest downward movement of any party.

### Taxation

Higher wealth taxation and related measures produce a Taxation Score of 68.

## Why This Produces a Final Score of 48

Substantial increase in state control over investment, production, and resource allocation. Average of Regulation 35 and Taxation 68 = 48.
