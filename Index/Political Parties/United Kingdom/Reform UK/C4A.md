### Reform UK
**Brief Overview**  
Reform UK places greater emphasis on traditional meritocratic selection, skills-based pathways, and resistance to policies that prioritise group-based outcomes over individual achievement.

**Impact on Social Determinism – Expanded Analysis**
- Support for expanding selective education and grammar schools.
- Scepticism toward diversity, equity, and inclusion initiatives that may dilute merit-based selection.
- Emphasis on practical skills, apprenticeships, and traditional routes to advancement.

**Why This Produces a Final Score of 32**  
Reform UK’s platform leans toward preserving and expanding mechanisms that reward higher achievement and selection, resulting in a moderately higher level of social determinism than the current British average.
