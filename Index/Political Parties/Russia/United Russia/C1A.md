# United Russia – Cultural 1A (Foundation)

**Score: 13**

**Final Score: 13**

United Russia and the broader presidential vertical present themselves as the defenders of Russian sovereignty, civilizational identity, historical continuity and the state’s capacity to protect the nation against external hostility. Their core aim is the preservation and strengthening of a sovereign Russian state capable of defending its interests, values and way of life.

#### Moral Justification
* Sovereignty is treated as existential and non-negotiable.
* Civilizational distinctiveness of Russia and resistance to Western imposition of alien norms are regularly invoked.
* Historical continuity, traditional values (family, faith, patriotism) and unity of the people under strong leadership are presented as sources of strength.
* Performance in delivering stability, security and great-power status remains important.
* External threat and the need for national solidarity function as organising frames.

The language is state-centric, civilizational and sovereignty-focused. Higher claims about Russia’s historical destiny and traditional values appear, but they are tightly fused with the practical requirements of state power and popular support for the leadership. The formation presents itself as the indispensable guardian of Russia’s existence as a sovereign civilization-state.

#### Why this produces a final score of 13
Transcendent sources (civilizational mission, historical destiny, traditional values) are present but heavily subordinated to and fused with state interest and popular support for strong leadership. The overall profile remains strongly mundane/state-centric, with a civilizational overlay that does not displace popular-sovereignty and state-interest primacy.
