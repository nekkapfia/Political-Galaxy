# SPD – Cultural 4A (Hierarchy)

### SPD
**Brief Overview**  
The SPD supports reducing early disadvantages through expanded early childhood education, all-day schooling, and measures to improve permeability between tracks, while generally working within the existing tracking framework.

**Why This Produces a Final Score of 24**  
The SPD’s approach of incremental improvement and expanded support within the current tracking system aligns closely with Germany’s present level of social determinism.
