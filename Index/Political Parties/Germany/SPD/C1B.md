The SPD has experienced declining Orthodoxy in recent decades as it has struggled with internal divisions between traditionalists and modernisers.

### Mechanisms of Protection and Rejection – Expanded Analysis
- Internal factionalism between left and centre has reduced party discipline.
- The party has revised traditional positions significantly (Agenda 2010, Hartz reforms).
- It maintains strong rejection of cooperation with the AfD.
- It supports progressive positions on social issues and climate policy.

The SPD shows moderate Orthodoxy due to internal divisions and a history of major policy reversals. Its Tribalism is moderate, focused on defending social democratic and progressive values while rejecting the far right.
