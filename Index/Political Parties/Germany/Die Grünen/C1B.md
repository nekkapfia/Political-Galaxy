# Die Grünen – Cultural 1B (Dogmatism)

**Score: 75**

Die Grünen maintain relatively high internal discipline around their core environmental and progressive values.

### Mechanisms of Protection and Rejection – Expanded Analysis
- Strong internal culture around climate, gender, and social issues.
- The party has moderated some positions in government (e.g., on nuclear power and debt brake).
- It strongly rejects cooperation with the AfD and maintains clear progressive boundaries.
- It promotes a cosmopolitan and ecological vision of German identity.

Die Grünen show relatively high Orthodoxy around their core ideological positions. Their Tribalism is high in rejecting nationalist and conservative alternatives, particularly on climate, migration, and identity issues.
