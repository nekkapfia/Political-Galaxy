# AfD – Cultural 1B (Dogmatism)

**Score: 84**

The AfD maintains very high internal discipline around its nationalist and anti-immigration positions.

### Mechanisms of Protection and Rejection – Expanded Analysis
- Strong internal discipline and rejection of deviation from the party line.
- Clear and consistent rejection of the post-2015 immigration consensus and much of the established party system.
- Strong emphasis on national identity and opposition to supranational (especially EU) frameworks.
- The party has undergone several internal purges to maintain ideological coherence.

The AfD shows very high Orthodoxy and very high Tribalism. It maintains strict internal control and strongly rejects the dominant post-war and post-2015 consensus on immigration, EU integration, and national identity.
