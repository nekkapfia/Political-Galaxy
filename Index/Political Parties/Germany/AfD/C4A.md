### AfD
**Brief Overview**  
The AfD generally favours traditional selection mechanisms, strong academic standards, and resistance to policies that prioritise group identity or migration background over individual merit.

**Why This Produces a Final Score of 31**  
The AfD’s positions lean toward preserving stronger selection mechanisms and placing less priority on actively narrowing birth-based quality gaps, producing one of the higher scores among current parties.
