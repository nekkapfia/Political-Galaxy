# AfD – Cultural 3B (Xenophobia)

**Score: 60**

### AfD Final Score: 3A 0 | 3B 60

The AfD maintains ethnic openness in formal policy while advocating the strongest cultural expectations for membership among major parties.

#### Allocation Mechanisms Explained Analysis
- Calls for significantly stricter integration requirements, including deeper commitment to German *Leitkultur* and values.
- Opposes the 2024 citizenship law and advocates reversing many of its provisions.
- Frames large-scale culturally distant migration as a threat to social cohesion and national identity.
- Supports making naturalisation more difficult and continuing stricter expectations for full membership.
- Explicitly rejects ethnic criteria for membership while strongly emphasising cultural alignment.

The AfD combines absolute rejection of ethnic criteria with the clearest argument that German membership should require meaningful adoption of German cultural standards, particularly for those from culturally distant backgrounds. This produces the highest 3B score among the major parties and reflects a strong preference for cultural alignment as a central condition of Endos status.
