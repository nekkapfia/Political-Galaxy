# AfD – Cultural 1A (Foundation)

**Score: 12**

**Final Score: 12**

The AfD presents itself as the party of national self-preservation and democratic reassertion. Its core aims are an immediate halt to irregular immigration, the deportation of those without legal status, and the defence of the concrete interests and cultural continuity of the existing German population.

#### Moral Justification
* Immigration policy is justified by the right of the German people to control their borders and by the concrete costs and security risks imposed on citizens.
* National interest of living Germans and reciprocity function as organising principles.
* Elite, judicial, media and European constraints are criticised as overrides of democratic will.
* Cultural continuity is defended as the inheritance and cohesion of the existing population rather than as sacred authority.
* Recent internal adjustments have softened certain terms while retaining the substance of demographic and cultural self-preservation.

The language is demos-centred, reciprocal and anti-elite. Higher-law or sacred claims are almost entirely absent. The party presents itself as the vehicle for ordinary Germans whose interests have been subordinated to migration and elite projects.

#### Why this produces a final score of 12
The transcendent source is effectively absent. Moral authority is derived overwhelmingly from popular sovereignty and the concrete interests of living German citizens, producing an extremely strong mundane lean.
