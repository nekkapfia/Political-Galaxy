The CDU/CSU maintains relatively high internal discipline and a strong commitment to its traditional principles of economic stability, EU integration, and moderate conservatism.

### Mechanisms of Protection and Rejection – Expanded Analysis
- Strong party discipline and leadership structures limit internal deviation.
- The party has shown willingness to revise positions (e.g., on nuclear power and same-sex marriage) when politically necessary.
- It maintains clear rejection of cooperation with the AfD.
- It defends core post-war frameworks (EU integration, fiscal responsibility, constitutional order).

The CDU/CSU combines relatively high Orthodoxy with moderate Tribalism. It protects its core principles internally while rejecting cooperation with the far right. Its Tribalism is focused more on institutional stability than on strong cultural nationalism.
